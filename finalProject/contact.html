Our Story

Our close relationships with so many local farms allow us to offer you a wide variety of locally grown produce that is harvested daily, hand-packed and personally delivered to your home or office.

In 1995, Bountiful Foods opened as a farmers market to offer fresh organic lacally grown foods to for the pubic in the Carlsbad, CA area. It was the city‚Äôs first health food store and the mission was simple: to offer fresh oraganic locally grown fruits and produce to empower people to regain control of their health.

Our first store was just 1,200 square feet and offered a modest selection of organic produce grown in the area. Growing over the years, our commitment to Clean, healthy food has never wavered. Our Food Philosophy ensures that you can shop and take home the freshest ingredients to put on your table.

In March 2020, a loyal customer and local business leader purchased Bountiful Foods with the vision to take fresh organic produce to more communities across Southern California and surrounding areas. Supported by investors, our original founders, local advocates, and previous Bountiful Foods Team Members, the team will begin the renewed mission to improve lives by making healthy food available to everyone and bringing our unique Food Philosophy back to the communities we serve.
